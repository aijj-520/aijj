Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Applies to all AIJJ apps including AIJJ Diary and the AIJJ productivity suite

The short version: we don't collect your data. All AI processing happens on your device. We have no servers storing your personal information, content, or usage history.

1. Who We Are

AIJJ LLC is a Wyoming limited liability company that develops and distributes privacy-first AI-powered mobile applications through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Our contact email is [email protected].

2. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to all mobile applications developed and published by AIJJ LLC, including AIJJ Diary and all apps in the AIJJ productivity suite (Resume Builder AI, Cover Letter AI, Email Reply AI, and all other AIJJ-branded apps).

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We want to be completely transparent. The following types of data are never collected by our apps or transmitted to our servers:

  • Any text, content, or documents you create or input into our apps
  • Photos or images you attach or upload
  • AI-generated output or analysis results
  • Voice recordings or transcriptions
  • Your name, email address, or any account information (no accounts required)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers used for tracking or advertising
  • Analytics, usage behavior, or session data
  • Crash reports containing personal information

4. How On-Device AI Works

All AIJJ apps use artificial intelligence models that run entirely on your device. When you input content and request AI processing:

  • Your input is processed locally by an AI model stored on your device
  • No data is sent to our servers or any third-party AI service
  • The AI model operates completely offline after the initial installation download
  • Results are computed and stored only in your device's local storage

This is architecturally different from cloud AI services. We are technically incapable of viewing your content because it is never transmitted to us.

5. Local Data Storage

All data you create — content, history, settings, preferences — is stored locally on your device using platform-standard secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore encrypted storage). This data:

  • Never leaves your device except through standard platform backup (iCloud, Google Backup), which you control
  • Is protected by your device passcode, Face ID, or fingerprint authentication
  • Is permanently and irrecoverably deleted when you uninstall the app

6. No Third-Party Tracking

Our apps do not include:

  • Advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • Analytics frameworks (no Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • Social media tracking pixels or SDKs
  • Crash reporting tools that transmit personal data

We do not sell, rent, share, or monetize user data in any form, because we do not possess any user data to share.

7. App Store Transactions

If you purchase a subscription or one-time purchase through our apps, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). AIJJ LLC receives only aggregate, anonymized revenue information from these platforms. Your payment details, billing address, and financial information are never accessible to us.

8. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used our apps in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us at [email protected].

9. International Users

Our apps are available globally. Because all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers, there is no cross-border data transfer by AIJJ LLC. Your data stays in your possession, on your device, in your country.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify users through an in-app notice. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
