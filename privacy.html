Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · Applies to all aijj apps including Ponder

The short version: we don't collect your data. All AI processing happens on your device. We have no servers storing your personal information or journal entries.

1. Who We Are

AIJJ LLC is a Wyoming limited liability company that develops privacy-focused mobile applications. Our contact email is [email protected].

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We want to be completely transparent. The following types of data are never collected by our apps or transmitted to our servers:

  • Journal entries, notes, or any text you write
  • Photos or images you attach to entries
  • Mood data or emotional analysis results
  • Voice recordings or transcriptions
  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers used for tracking
  • Analytics or usage behavior data

3. How On-Device AI Works

Our apps use artificial intelligence models that run entirely on your device (your iPhone or Android phone). When you write a journal entry and request AI analysis:

  • The text is processed locally by an AI model stored on your device
  • No data is sent to our servers or any third-party AI service
  • The AI model operates completely offline after installation
  • Results are stored only in your device's local encrypted storage

4. Local Storage

All your data — journal entries, photos, mood history, settings — is stored locally on your device using the platform's secure storage mechanisms (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). This data:

  • Never leaves your device except through standard iOS/Android backup (iCloud, Google Backup) which you control
  • Is protected by your device passcode / Face ID / Touch ID
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

5. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not include any advertising SDKs, analytics frameworks, crash-reporting tools that transmit personal data, or social media tracking pixels in our apps. We do not sell, rent, or share data with third parties because we do not have your data to share.

6. App Store Purchases

If you purchase a subscription, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We receive only aggregate financial information from these platforms. Your payment details are never accessible to us.

7. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our apps, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify users through an in-app notice.
