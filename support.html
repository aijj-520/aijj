Privacy & Data
Is my data private and secure?
Yes. All data you create in any AIJJ app is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers. We have no servers that receive your content. All AI processing happens entirely on your device — offline and private.
Do AIJJ apps require an internet connection?
No. All AIJJ apps work fully offline after installation. The AI model runs on your device without any network connection. An internet connection is only needed for the initial AI model download on first launch.
Does AIJJ collect any analytics or usage data?
No. Our apps contain no analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or crash reporting tools that transmit personal data. We do not track how you use our apps. We do not sell data to third parties because we do not collect it.
Where is my data stored?
All data — your content, settings, history — is stored locally on your device using iOS or Android secure storage. It is protected by your device passcode, Face ID, or fingerprint. It is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.
Subscriptions & Billing
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play — not through us. To cancel on iPhone: go to Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → find the AIJJ app → tap Cancel. On Android: open Google Play → tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
How do I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple or Google. For App Store purchases, visit reportaproblem.apple.com. For Google Play, visit the Google Play Help Center. Apple generally offers refunds within 90 days of purchase. If you have trouble, email us and we'll do our best to help.
Why does the price look different in my country?
Apple and Google automatically adjust prices for local currencies, taxes (like VAT), and regional purchasing power. The base subscription is set in USD, and the platforms convert this to local pricing. We don't manually set prices per country.
Can I use the same subscription on multiple devices?
Yes. As long as you use the same Apple ID or Google account, your subscription is valid across all your devices. Restore purchases from within the app's settings if a new device doesn't recognize your subscription.
Using the Apps
How do I transfer my data to a new phone?
Use iCloud Backup (iOS) or Google Backup (Android) before switching devices. When you restore your new device and reinstall the AIJJ app, your data will be restored as part of the system backup. Make sure backup is enabled before switching.
What happens if I delete the app?
All locally stored data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app. Since we have no servers, there is no backup on our end. Use iCloud or Google Backup before uninstalling if you want to preserve your data.
The AI output doesn't look right — what should I do?
AI-generated content is a starting point, not a finished product. Try rephrasing your input with more specific details. If the output is consistently poor, please email us with a description of what you entered and what you expected — this helps us improve our prompts.

AI-Generated Content
Can I use AI-generated content for professional purposes?
Yes. Our apps are designed for professional use cases like resumes, emails, and business writing. Always review and edit AI output before submitting professionally — AI can make errors, and you are responsible for the final content you send.
Is AI-generated content copyrighted?
The legal status of AI-generated content varies by jurisdiction and is still evolving. Generally, content you generate using our apps is yours to use. We recommend adding original thought and editing to any AI output you intend to publish or submit.
What AI model do the apps use?
Our apps use lightweight AI models optimized to run locally on mobile devices. The specific model may vary by app and platform. All models run entirely on your device — no data is sent to any AI provider's servers.
