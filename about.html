About AIJJ

We build tools that
work for you —
not against you

AIJJ LLC is an independent software company developing AI-powered productivity apps for global markets. We believe the best AI is the kind that runs on your device, respects your privacy, and gets out of your way.

"Small, focused tools powered by on-device AI — built for people who value privacy, clarity, and craft."

Most AI apps send your data to the cloud, lock you into bloated platforms, and grow more invasive over time. We build the opposite: lightweight, single-purpose tools where all AI processing runs locally on your device. No servers receive your content. No algorithms profile your behavior. Your data stays yours.

Business Model

How we operate

📱

Mobile App Distribution

All AIJJ apps are distributed exclusively through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store across all major developed markets worldwide.

💳

Subscription Revenue

Apps are monetized through in-app subscriptions managed entirely by Apple and Google. All transactions are processed by the platform and disbursed to AIJJ LLC.

🌍

Global Markets

We target developed markets across North America, Europe, Japan, and South Korea. All apps are fully localized in English, Japanese, Korean, German, French, Spanish, and Portuguese.

🔒

On-Device AI Architecture

All AI processing runs locally on the user's device using lightweight models optimized for mobile. No cloud processing of user content. No server-side data storage.
