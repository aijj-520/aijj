Frequently Asked Questions

Is my journal data private and secure?
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers. We have no servers that receive your journal entries. On-device AI processes your text entirely within your phone.
Does Ponder require an internet connection?
No. Ponder works entirely offline after installation. The AI model runs on your device without needing a network connection. An internet connection is only needed for the initial AI model download on first launch.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed entirely through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). To cancel: on iPhone, go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions, find Ponder, and tap Cancel Subscription. On Android, open Google Play → Menu → Subscriptions.
How do I transfer my data to a new phone?
Use iCloud Backup (iOS) or Google Backup (Android) to migrate your data. When you restore from backup on your new device and install Ponder, your entries will be restored along with the rest of your app data.
What happens if I delete the app?
All locally stored data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app. Since we have no servers, there is no backup on our end. Make sure to use iCloud or Google Backup before uninstalling if you want to keep your data.
I found a bug — how do I report it?
Please email [email protected] with a description of the issue, your device model, iOS/Android version, and steps to reproduce the problem if possible. We aim to fix critical bugs within 48 hours.
Is there a refund policy?
Refunds are handled by Apple or Google through their respective platforms. Apple generally offers refunds within 90 days of purchase — visit reportaproblem.apple.com. For Google Play, visit the Play Store help center.

We typically respond within 24–48 hours on business days. For urgent issues, please include "URGENT" in your email subject line.
